      Circle Attempts to draw a circle at the current pen position, either filled or outlined, depending on the specified parameter. Ensures the radius is valid and checks if the circle fits within the canvas boundaries. If the radius is invalid or the circle exceeds the canvas size, a warning is logged, and drawing is skipped.
